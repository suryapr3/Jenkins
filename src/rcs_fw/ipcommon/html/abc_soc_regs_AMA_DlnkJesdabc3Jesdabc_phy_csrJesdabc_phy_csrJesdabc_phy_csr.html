Field ln_clk_div_rate
Transmit input word clock speed indicator. Used when ick_pcs_txword_l0 is faster than the expected clock frequency.
Example: ick_pcs_txword_l0 is 1 GHz in 8b PCIe Gen1, but the expected clock frequency is 250 MHz.

3'd1: Transmit every 2 cycles
3'd2: Transmit every 4 cycles
3'd3: Transmit every 8 cycles
