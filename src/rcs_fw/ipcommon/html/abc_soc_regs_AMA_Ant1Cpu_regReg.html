Stationary Vector Slecet
Selects between one of two stationary vector bases (0: default, 1: alternative).
The reset vector and the memory error exception handler vector addresses are fixed offsets from the stationary vector base.
The two stationary vector bases are configurable. The StatVectorSel pin must be held stable for at least 10 CLK cycles before the falling edge of BReset, and for 10 CLK cycles after the falling edge of BReset.
