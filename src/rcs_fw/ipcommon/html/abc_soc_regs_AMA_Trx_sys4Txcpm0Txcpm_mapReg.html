+0x0000000c Register(32 bit) DLINK_MHI
Downlink Measurement and Alarm Hold or Initialize
Options for Measurement and Alarm during Downlink ==0
-Downlink Measurement Hold or Initialize:
When the Downlink Switches High to Low, this bit determines if the in-progress window measurements are maintained or are reinitialized to zero when the Downlink goes high (active)
0 == Reinitialize Widow Measurement and start again.
1 == Hold the in progress values and continue with the window measurement

- Downlink Alarm Hold or Initialize
0 == An asserted alarm remains asserted when the link goes low.
1 == An asserted alarm is set low whne the link goes low.
